About Us

About Rotaract District Organisation 3011

Rotaract District 3011 is dedicated to fostering leadership and community service among young professionals and students. We focus on impactful projects that address local and global issues, creating positive change in our communities.

Our district supports initiatives that promote personal development, professional growth, and community engagement. Join us in making a difference through diverse activities and projects.

How We Work?

Project Launch

Firstly, a project is launched based on the social problems in the society.

Survey Conducted

A survey is conducted in the villages and urban areas in the vicinity.

Volunteer Teams

We create separate volunteer teams for each of the village.

Community Mobilisation

Communities are mobilised and awareness is kindled.

Campaign & Execution

Campaigns are launched and projects are executed.

Feedback & Improvement

Feedback is taken to ensure improvisation.
